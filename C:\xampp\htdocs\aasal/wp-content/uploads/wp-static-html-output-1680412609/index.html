Welcome to Aasal International!

  • About Aasal International
  • Who We Are?
AASAL INTERNATIONAL is Manufacturer, Exporter, Supplier and Wholesaler of Indian Spices seeds,Ground Spices,Oil seeds and Agro Products.
We have experience of more than 10 years in domestic trading in Gujarat and in India. We are Located at Gandhinagar; Gujarat, India.
We are Government of India recognized Exporter and also registered with Spices Board of India and APEDA.
Aasal International, as a group of young and enthusiastic individuals, Our goal of being a global leader in spices trading is ambitious and admirable. By prioritizing the export of Indian spices and other handy made products, we have identified a smart strategy for capitalizing on India’s reputation for producing high-quality spices. With our expertise as analysts and researchers, along with an up-to-date global food market database
We have the resources to make informed decisions about which products to prioritize and which markets to target.
